1. Structural Data Architecture

We leverage localized, secure browser-level state engines to optimize tracking matrix stability without compromising personal student safety. All data remains exclusively on your device.

  • Cross-Session Preferences: User display names and interface configurations are bound locally via browser storage scripts. No data is sent to external servers.

2. Local Device Persistence

To eliminate redundant verification overheads and database dependencies, our nodes maintain unified local data. Authentication barriers (like Firebase) have been completely removed. Your progress belongs to you and stays securely within your browser's local cache.
